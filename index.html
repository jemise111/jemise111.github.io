About Me

Hello! My name is Jesse and I'm a web developer. I'm a recent graduate of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program, a 12-week bootcamp that teaches the fundamentals of front-end and back-end development. During my time there I learned about all aspects of the web application building process, from design to deployment.

I previously worked at Balyasny Asset Management, a multi-strategy hedge fund, where I researched equities in the tech and internet sectors and gave investment recommendations. During my time at Balyasny I met executives behind some of the most interesting and powerful projects on the web today. As my interest grew I felt it shifting from the finance side to the product side, so I decided to transition to the web development world.

Please see projects I have worked on below and feel free to contact me any time!
